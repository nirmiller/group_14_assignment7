Game Functionality: 
    - Use WASD keys to move around screen
    - Use space bar to shoot 
    - If hit by astroid player is killed 
    
Rules of Play:
    - Don't get hit by astroid 
    - Shoot astroids to avoid being killed 
    
Win and Lose Conditions: 
    - There is not a strict win conditions, wining is more so based on time alive. 
    - You lose if the astroid hits you and you are on your third life.  
    
Classes 
    - Astroid.cs (Nir) 
        - Astroid movement and rotation
        - Collision detection
        - Astroid's life time
    - AstroidSpawn.cs (Nir) 
        - Difficulty scale 
        - Each frame, every asteroid is dequeued, updated via Animate(), and re-enqueued only if still alive — dead 
        ones are counted as kills and discarded.
        - collision reporting 
        - randomly spawns new astroids off screen 
        - resets astroids and timer 
    - HUD.cs (Chris) 
        - Tracks if game is active or over
        - Increments score by 1 every second 
        - Tracks and updates the lives left 
        - Game over screen
